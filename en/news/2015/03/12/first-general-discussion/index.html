First General Discussion

Written by Quintus on 2015-03-12.

Official announcement of the first General Discussion of the TSC development team.

Everybody is invited!

The 1st General Discussion of the entire TSC team has been announced officially today. It will be taken out on 2015-03-21 20:00:00 UTC in IRC on the Freenode IRC chat network. A large number of team members have already announced they’re going to join the meeting, and as several important things are going to be discussed and probably being decided on, everybody is invited to join and provide their thoughts to the team.

For all details and modalities, please read the announcement in full. That file will be updated while more topics and speakers are suggested.

Quintus
TSC project lead.
